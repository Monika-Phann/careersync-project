# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 15, 2024

At CareerSync, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our mentoring platform.

By using CareerSync, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you register on CareerSync as a mentee, we may collect:

- Full name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Date of birth  
- Gender  
- Profile picture (optional)  
- Educational institution and school ID  
- Student status (student or working professional)  
- Optional workplace information  

### 1.2 Usage Information
We automatically collect information about how you interact with our platform, including:

- Session booking and attendance records  
- Communication between you and mentors  
- Platform usage patterns and preferences  
- Device information and IP addresses  

### 1.3 Cookies and Tracking
We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

- Enhance your experience  
- Analyze platform usage  
- Provide personalized content  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery:** Facilitate mentoring connections, schedule sessions, and enable communication  
- **Account Management:** Create and authenticate your account and provide customer support  
- **Matching:** Connect mentees with appropriate mentors based on expertise, goals, and preferences  
- **Communication:** Send session confirmations, reminders, platform updates, and notifications  
- **Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve our services  
- **Safety:** Verify identities, prevent fraud, and ensure platform security  
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with applicable laws and regulations

## 3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 3.1 Within the Platform
Your profile information may be visible to other users as necessary for mentoring connections.  
- Mentees can view mentor profiles  
- Mentors can view information about mentees who request sessions  

### 3.2 Third-Party Service Providers
We may share your information with trusted third parties who assist us with:

- Hosting and maintaining the platform  
- Processing payments  
- Sending communications  
- Analyzing data and usage patterns  

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of CareerSync, our users, or others.

### 3.4 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest  
- Secure authentication and access controls  
- Regular security assessments and updates  
- Employee training on data protection  

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

When you deactivate your account, we will delete or anonymize your information within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required by law.
