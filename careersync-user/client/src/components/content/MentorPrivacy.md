# Mentor Privacy Policy  
**Last Updated:** December 15, 2024  

At our mentorship platform, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you register and use our platform as a mentor.

By registering and using our platform, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
When you register as a mentor, we may collect:

- **Account Information:** Email address, password (encrypted), profile picture  
- **Personal Information:** Full name, date of birth, phone number, location  
- **Professional Information:** Current position, company, years of experience, areas of expertise  
- **Educational Background:** Degrees, certifications, academic credentials  
- **Verification Documents:** IDs, CV, portfolio, or professional licenses (if required)  
- **Communication Data:** Messages and interactions on the platform  

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
We automatically collect certain information when you use the platform:

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent  
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers  
- **Log Data:** Access times, error logs, performance data  
- **Cookies and Tracking:** Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies  

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
We may receive information from trusted third parties, including:

- Social media platforms (if you connect accounts)  
- Professional verification services  
- Payment processors (for financial transactions)  
- Background check providers (if applicable)

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your personal information for the following purposes:

- **Account Management:** Create and maintain your mentor account  
- **Matching Services:** Connect you with suitable mentees based on expertise and preferences  
- **Communication:** Send notifications, updates, and respond to inquiries  
- **Platform Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience  
- **Safety and Security:** Prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure platform safety  
- **Payment Processing:** Handle transactions and compensation (if applicable)  
- **Legal Compliance:** Meet legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations  
- **Marketing (Optional):** Send promotional materials with your consent

## 4. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

- **Encryption:** Data in transit secured using SSL/TLS protocols  
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access and authentication measures  
- **Password Protection:** Hashed and encrypted passwords  
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments and vulnerability testing  
- **Secure Storage:** Data stored on protected servers with monitoring  

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

---

## 5. Data Retention
We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

- Maintain your mentor account and provide services  
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements  
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements  
- Prevent fraud and ensure platform security  

After account deactivation, data may be anonymized or retained in aggregated form. Backup data may be retained for up to **90 days**.

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you  
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information  
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data  
- **Data Portability:** Request a structured, machine-readable copy of your data  
- **Opt-Out:** Unsubscribe from marketing communications  
- **Cookie Management:** Control cookie preferences through browser settings  
- **Object to Processing:** Object to certain types of data processing  
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw previously given consent at any time  

To exercise these rights, contact us at **privacy@mentorplatform.com**. We will respond within **30 days**.
